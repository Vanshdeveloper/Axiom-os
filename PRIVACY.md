# Axiom OS - Privacy Statement 🛡️

Axiom OS is built with a **"Local-First"** philosophy. Your data is your own, and we never have access to it.

### **1. Data Ownership**
All tasks, notes, and research conducted through Axiom OS are stored directly within your private **Notion workspace**. Axiom OS does not have a central database where your information is collected or stored.

### **2. Proxy Security**
Axiom OS uses **Google Gemini AI** and **Puppeteer** to process and summarize your research. This information is sent to Google's Generative AI API using **your own private API key**.

### **3. Third-Party Services**
- **Notion API**: Used to save and retrieve your notes, tasks, and financial logs. 
- **Telegram Bot API**: Used to deliver commands, notifications, and process voice messages.
- **Google Generative AI**: Used for research synthesis, voice transcription, and intent extraction.

### **4. Voice Audio Processing**
Axiom OS processes voice messages using an **"Extraction-Only"** approach. Audio files are briefly accessed via your private Google Gemini API key for transcription and intent extraction. We do not store, train on, or retain your audio data.

### **5. Financial Data Privacy**
Axiom OS logs your expenses directly to your private Notion Finance Database. We do not process, analyze, or view your spending habits for any reason other than delivering the summary reports you request via the bot.

### **6. Local Hosting**
Axiom OS runs **locally on your machine** or private server. No telemetry or tracking scripts are embedded in the software.

---
Your productivity should never come at the cost of your privacy. 🦾
